This is an honest, research-backed analysis of where Artificial Intelligence actually stands today (February 2026), how far we are from Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), and what the mainstream narrative gets wrong.

## Key Findings At A Glance

1. **AI is a productivity tool** - extraordinarily powerful, not intelligent
2. **AGI is not close** - 76% of AI researchers say scaling LLMs alone is "unlikely" to reach AGI
3. **LLM loops won't get us there** - model collapse is a proven, published problem
4. **China's constraint may be an advantage** - DeepSeek proved efficiency beats brute force
5. **AI is a perfect propaganda machine** - and 90% of users don't question its output
6. **The only defense is critical thinking** - which AI is quietly eroding at scale
